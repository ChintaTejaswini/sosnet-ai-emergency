Emergency First Aid

Immediate, clear instructions for life-critical situations.

ecg_heart

CPR

Cardiac Arrest

1.

Check responsiveness & breathing.

2.

Call Emergency Services (911).

3.

Push hard and fast in center of chest.

blood_pressure

Bleeding

Wound Control

1.

Stay Calm & Wear Gloves.

2.

Apply direct pressure to wound.

3.

Keep limb elevated above heart.

local_fire_department

Burns

Heat Exposure

1.

Cool with running water for 20 mins.

2.

Remove jewelry/clothing (if not stuck).

3.

Cover loosely with plastic wrap.

home_health

Fracture

Bone Injury

1.

Do not attempt to realign bone.

2.

Immobilize with a splint/sling.

3.

Apply ice to reduce swelling.

pest_control

Snake Bite

Venom Control

1.

Keep victim calm & immobile.

2.

Position bite below heart level.

3.

Do not cut or suck the wound.

wb_sunny

Heat Stroke

Overheating

1.

Move person to a cool area.

2.

Cool with wet cloths or misting.

3.

Fanning for evaporative cooling.

warning
